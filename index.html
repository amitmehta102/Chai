About Chai Adda

Chai Adda is not just a tea café — it is an emotion deeply rooted in Indian culture. From early morning newspaper chai to late-night college discussions, chai has always been the heart of conversations.

We prepare our chai using premium tea leaves, fresh milk, and authentic Indian spices. Every cup is brewed with care to deliver warmth, consistency, and comfort.

Whether you love kadak chai, herbal blends, or modern fusion flavors, Chai Adda is a place where people connect and memories are brewed — one cup at a time.
